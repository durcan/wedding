Travel

By Plane

The nearest airports to Pasadena are Los Angeles International Airport (LAX) and Bob Hope Airport in Burbank (BUR). We recommend guests to rent cars or take taxis or Supershuttle to their hotels.

BUR

Bob Hope Airport

  • Super Shuttle 800-258-3826; fee: $23 (one way); reservation required
  • Estimated taxi fare: $55 (one way)
LAX

Los Angeles International Airport

  • Super Shuttle 800-258-3826; fee: $25 (one way); reservation required
  • Estimated taxi fare: $80 (one way)
  • LAX Flyaway Bus is also an inexpensive way to get from LAX to Union Station in downtown Los Angeles (See "By Train" for instructions): $9.50 (one way including Metro Gold line one-way fare).

By Car

For guests driving, hotels are conveniently located near the CA-134 East / Interstate 210 freeway (Fair Oaks Avenue or Lake Avenue Exits) and 110 North freeway (becomes Arroyo Parkway).

By Train

For guests coming into Union Station from Amtrak or the LAX Flyaway Bus, please take the Metro Gold Line from Union Station to the Memorial Park station in Pasadena.

Lodging

Pasadena has numerous accommodations options. However, we anticipate that there may be a limited number of hotel rooms available in anticipation of the Breeder's Cup in Santa Anita the following week. Please book your rooms as soon as possible.

The bride and groom will be staying at the Westin Pasadena, but we also recommend the Marriott Courtyard (where we have obtained a very reasonable group rate) and the Hilton Pasadena. All hotels are within walking distance from the wedding venue.
